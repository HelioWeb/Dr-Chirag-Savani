About Dr Chirag

Chirag Savani, MD, is a board-certified Neurologist who came to Health First from his Clinical Neurophysiology Fellowship where he studied the combined Electroencephalogram (EEG) and Electromyography (EMG) track at the University of South Florida in Tampa, Florida. He served his Neurology Residency where he was also the Research Chief Resident at the University of South Florida in Tampa, Florida.

Dr. Savani is licensed to practice medicine in Florida. He has his Educational Commission for Foreign Medical Graduates (ECFMG) certification.
